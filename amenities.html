Septic & Dump Station

Convenient Dump Station Access

30-100 feet from every site

This is one of our most appreciated amenities! Most RV parks require you to drive across the property to dump your tanks. At Back40, the dump station is never more than 100 feet from your site. For many sites, it's just 30-50 feet away.

Why This Matters

  • No need to disconnect, drive, and reconnect
  • Quick dumps at your convenience
  • Especially helpful for extended stays
  • Saves time and hassle

Septic Clean-Out Station Features

  • Available 24/7 for registered guests

Trail Access

Walk or Ride Directly from Your RV Site

The #1 reason outdoor enthusiasts choose Back40: direct trail access. No need to trailer your bikes or drive to trailheads. Park your RV and start your adventure.

What's Accessible from the Park

Back 40 Trail System

40+ miles of flowing XC mountain bike singletrack with machine-built berms and rollers. Gravel base with technical rocky sections. Main loop: 22.6 miles. Perfect for experienced mountain bikers.

Little Sugar Trails

50 miles of purpose-built mountain bike trails. Open 5 AM - 10 PM daily. Multiple loops and difficulty levels. Perfect for all skill levels and families.

Regional Trail Systems

250+ miles of trails within 30-minute drive. Razorback Greenway (36 mi paved trail). Blowing Springs Loop (7 mi scenic). Hiking, cycling, and gravel riding options.

NEW in 2025: OZ Trails Bike Park

20+ miles of gravity trails. First lift-served bike park in Northwest Arkansas. Opening Spring 2025. Located at junction of Bella Vista and Bentonville.

Direct access to 100+ miles of trails

What We Don't Have (And Why)

No Bathhouse Facilities

Back40 RV Park is designed for self-contained RVs with their own bathroom and shower facilities. We do not have a bathhouse building on-site.

Why No Bathhouse?

This design choice benefits our guests:

The Benefits:

  • Lower Rates - $50/night vs. $60-80 at full-service parks
  • Quieter Atmosphere - Fewer people walking around
  • More Privacy - Use your own facilities anytime
  • Modern RV Focus - Designed for today's RVs
  • Outdoor Focus - Resources on trails, not buildings

Is This Right for You?

Our park is perfect if your RV has:

  • Full bathroom (toilet and shower)
  • Fresh water tank
  • Gray water holding tank
  • Black water holding tank

Most modern travel trailers, fifth wheels, and motorhomes are fully equipped and perfect for Back40.
