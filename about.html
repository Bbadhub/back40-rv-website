About Back40 RV Park

Your basecamp for Ozark trail adventures

Welcome to Back40 RV Park

Back40 RV Park was created by Mark Swart with a simple vision: provide trail enthusiasts with the perfect basecamp for exploring the incredible mountain biking and hiking trails of the Ozarks.

Located at 1373 Hickory Lane in Pineville, Missouri, our RV park offers something truly special - direct access to the legendary Back 40 Trail System. You can literally ride or hike from your RV site onto 40+ miles of world-class singletrack.

We understand what trail enthusiasts need: a quiet, well-maintained place to rest and recharge between adventures. That's why Back40 RV Park focuses on the essentials - reliable electrical hookups, convenient dump station access, water fill, and the peace and quiet you need after a long day on the trails.

Our Approach

We're not a big resort with pools and playgrounds. We're a small, well-maintained RV park designed specifically for self-contained RVs. This approach allows us to keep rates affordable, minimize environmental impact, and maintain the quiet, natural atmosphere that our guests appreciate.

Meet Mark Swart

Mark Swart is the owner and operator of Back40 RV Park. As both an outdoor enthusiast and experienced property manager, Mark understands what makes a great RV camping experience.

Mark personally oversees the park's operations, ensuring every guest has a positive experience. When you book with Back40, you'll communicate directly with Mark - no call centers, no automated systems. Just straightforward, personal service from someone who genuinely cares about your visit.

Mark is happy to share local knowledge about trail conditions, nearby attractions, and hidden gems in the Pineville and Bella Vista area. He's built relationships with the local trail community and stays informed about events, races, and the best times to visit different trail systems.

Trail Systems

  • Back 40 Trail System (on-site)

  • Bentonville Trail System (30 min)
  • OZ Trails Bike Park (30 min)

What Makes Back40 Different

We're not trying to be everything to everyone. We're focused on doing one thing exceptionally well.

Trail Access is Our Priority

Most RV parks advertise "nearby trails." We offer direct, on-site access to the Back 40 Trail System. Park your RV, grab your bike or boots, and go. It doesn't get more convenient.

Self-Contained Model

By focusing on self-contained RVs, we keep costs down, reduce infrastructure, and maintain a low-impact operation. It's better for the environment and better for your wallet.

Personal Service

When you call Back40, you talk to Mark. When you email, Mark responds. No automated systems or overseas call centers. Just direct communication with the owner.

Peace & Quiet

We enforce quiet hours and maintain a respectful atmosphere. After a long day on the trails, you'll appreciate the peaceful environment for rest and recovery.

Open Year-Round

Whether you're chasing spring wildflowers, fall colors, or winter solitude, Back40 is open 365 days a year. The trails are rideable almost year-round in the Ozarks.

Transparent Pricing

$50/night or $300/week. That's it. No resort fees, no amenity charges, no surprise costs. What we quote is what you pay.
