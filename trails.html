Back 40 Trail System

The legendary trail system that defines Bella Vista mountain biking—accessible right from our park.

Trail Stats

  • Distance: 40+ miles of singletrack
  • Main Loop: 22.6 miles (6-8 hours for experienced riders)
  • Elevation Gain: 2,162 feet
  • Surface: Gravel base with rocky, technical sections
  • Difficulty: Moderate to Challenging (XC territory)

Trail Features

Machine-built berms for high-speed cornering • Rollers and flow sections • Narrow singletrack through wooded areas • Technical rocky sections • Well-maintained by local trail crews

Trail Personality

The Back 40 delivers swooping, well-built XC singletrack that flows both uphill and downhill. The gravelly trail tread can get slippery in corners, keeping you honest. This is pure XC hammerhead territory, perfect for banging out miles.

Little Sugar Trails

Purpose-built mountain bike trails offering variety for all skill levels.

Trail Stats

  • Distance: 50 miles of purpose-built trails
  • Hours: Open 5 AM - 10 PM daily
  • Difficulty: Beginner to Advanced options
  • Surface: Varied—hardpack, gravel, some technical

Perfect For

  • Beginner to intermediate riders
  • Families with young cyclists
  • Riders wanting variety
  • First-time mountain bikers
  • Social group rides

Trail Features

Multiple loops and route options • Progressive difficulty (start easy, build skills) • Well-marked intersections • Scenic Ozark forest views

Hiking Trails

Back 40 Hiking Loop

The same trail system mountain bikers love is open to hikers—offering stunning Ozark scenery.

  • 22.6-mile full loop (or shorter segments)
  • 2,162 ft elevation gain
  • Allow 8-10 hours for full loop
  • Moderate to strenuous difficulty

Highlights:

Dense Ozark forest • Wooded ravines and rolling hills • Wildlife viewing • Seasonal wildflowers • Peaceful atmosphere

Blowing Springs Loop

One of the most scenic trails in Bella Vista

  • 7-mile loop, flowing singletrack
  • Moderate difficulty, 2-3 hour hike
  • Hanging bluff lines & moss-covered boulders
  • Scenic overlooks & creek crossings
Trail access from Back40 RV Park

Gravel Riding Routes

The Back 40 trail system itself is largely gravel-based, making it ideal for gravel bikes. Beyond that, the Ozarks offer hundreds of miles of scenic gravel roads.

What to Expect

  • Rolling hills and elevation changes
  • Quiet country roads
  • Minimal traffic
  • Stunning rural scenery
  • Mix of hardpack and loose gravel

Popular Routes

  • Rural roads around Pineville & Bella Vista
  • Arkansas and Missouri backroads
  • Self-guided route planning
  • Group rides by local cycling clubs

Seasonal Trail Guide

🌸 Spring (March-May)

Best For: Wildflowers, moderate temps
Conditions: May be muddy after rain—check conditions
Highlights: Spring blooms, fresh greenery
Avg Temps: 50-70°F

☀️ Summer (June-Aug)

Best For: Long days, full foliage
Conditions: Hot/humid—ride early morning
Highlights: Lush forest, extended daylight
Avg Temps: 75-95°F
Tip: Start rides by 8 AM

🍂 Fall (Sept-Nov)

Best For: Peak foliage, ideal temps, events
Conditions: Perfect—dry, firm tread
Highlights: Fall colors, Bikes Blues BBQ
Avg Temps: 55-75°F
Peak Color: Mid-October

❄️ Winter (Dec-Feb)

Best For: Solitude, quiet trails
Conditions: Cold but rideable
Highlights: Peaceful, no crowds
Avg Temps: 30-50°F
Tip: Layer up, enjoy solitude
